The Anthropological and Archaeological Database of Sepultures

THANADOS is a Digital Humanities research project on archaeologically and anthropologically investigated burials. The project started in June 2019. The region under study is present day Austria and the period we are dealing with are the Early Middle Ages (600 until 1100 AD). Currently it is work in progress. Selected and processed sites are available online. By the end of the project in 2021 this website will provide an online repository of all hitherto published Early Medieval cemeteries from the area of present day Austria.
...learn more

With this application you can:

  • explore the digital catalogues of cemeteries
  • navigate through interactive maps of the respective sites
  • query and search burial data from graves, human remains and finds
  • visualise the results on maps
  • analyse interactive charts of the cemeteries' data
  • download the data for further analyses

If you want to know more about the features please take a look at the manual section.
