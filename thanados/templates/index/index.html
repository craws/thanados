The objective of the project 'Medieval Deserted Villages [MDV]' is the extensive and detailed documentation and investigation of deserted villages in a larger historic landscape context, before the irrevocable loss of this rich cultural heritage. The main research questions are concerned with the localisation of further villages, the identification of certain causes of abandonment as well as the study of their internal structure and organisation.

This web application for the digital dissemination of the respective project results offers users to:

  • explore the digital catalogs of deserted villages
  • navigate through interactive maps of the respective sites
  • query and search data on the villages and its components
  • visualise the results on maps
  • analyse interactive charts of the villages' data
  • download the data for further analyses

It is connected to the OpenAtlas project and based on its data model. If you want to learn more take a look at the About section.
